About Amar Automats

Making available Automotive Small Components such as Special Insert Bolt, Light Insert Bolt, Nuts, Bolts, Screws and Hang Nuts that are known for their resistivity to wear & tear, rustproof nature, tough construction and dimensional perfectness...
